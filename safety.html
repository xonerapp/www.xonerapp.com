SAFETY STANDARDS

Xoner is committed to user safety and maintaining a respectful, lawful community. This page outlines our standards related to protecting minors and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

1) Age Requirement

Xoner is intended for adults. Users must be 18+. We do not allow content that sexualizes minors, or any attempt to engage with minors for sexual purposes.

2) Prohibited Content & Behavior (Zero Tolerance)

  • Any child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or CSAE content.
  • Any sexual content involving minors (real, implied, animated, AI-generated, or otherwise).
  • Grooming, solicitation, sexual extortion, trafficking, or any attempt to exploit minors.
  • Requests for sexual images of minors or links to such material.

3) Reporting & Enforcement

We take user reports seriously. When we become aware of suspected CSAM/CSAE, we take action which may include:

  • Immediate account restriction or permanent ban.
  • Preserving relevant logs or evidence as required by law.
  • Escalation to appropriate legal authorities where required.

4) How to Report a Safety Concern

If you believe someone is endangering a minor or attempting to share CSAM/CSAE content, report it immediately:

Email: support@xonerapp.com
Subject line suggestion: “Safety Report – Urgent”

5) External Reporting Options

If you are in immediate danger, contact your local emergency services. In the United States, you can also report suspected child exploitation to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) CyberTipline.
