Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Xoner handles your information when you use the app. By creating an account or using Xoner, you agree that your information may be collected and processed as described in this Policy.

Xoner is operated by Xoner App. For privacy-related questions or requests, you may contact us at support@xonerapp.com.

2. Information We Collect

Xoner may collect several types of information to provide and improve the service, including:

  • Profile details such as your name, age, gender and interests.
  • Photos and selfies for verification features.
  • Contact information such as email or phone number (if provided).
  • Location information to display nearby profiles.
  • Ratings you give or receive inside the app.
  • Device, usage and analytics data to maintain app performance.
  • Verification selfies: used solely for identity verification and deleted immediately after verification is completed.
  • Messages and chats: stored securely to allow users to view conversation history and deleted when users delete their chats.
  • Approximate location data: limited to city or region level. Xoner does not collect or track precise GPS location.
  • Usage & analytics data: aggregated and non-invasive metrics (such as downloads, swipes, matches and activity patterns) used to improve app performance and user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

Xoner uses collected information to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Display your profile to other users.
  • Support rating, matching and verification features.
  • Improve app performance and user experience.
  • Detect or prevent misuse, fraud or safety issues.
  • Provide customer support if needed.

4. Sharing of Information

Xoner does not sell your personal information. Information may be shared in limited situations such as with other users, service providers, or where required by law.

5. Your Choices

You may update or delete your profile information at any time or delete your account through the app.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your data, but no system can guarantee complete security.

7. Children’s Privacy

Xoner is strictly for users aged 18 and above.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

9. Data Retention

Xoner retains personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the service:

  • Account and profile data is retained while your account is active.
  • Messages are retained until deleted by users.
  • Verification selfies are deleted immediately after verification.
  • Analytics data is retained in aggregated form.

10. Data Storage & Transfers

Xoner processes and stores data on secure servers located in the United States. By using the App, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred to and processed in this region, subject to appropriate safeguards.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete or restrict the processing of your personal data. Requests can be made by contacting support@xonerapp.com.

12. Contact

For questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact support@xonerapp.com.
